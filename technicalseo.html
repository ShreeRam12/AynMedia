FAQs

What is the typical turnaround time for graphic design projects?

+

The turnaround time for graphic design projects depends on the scope and complexity of the project. However, we strive to deliver designs within a reasonable timeframe. We will discuss and agree upon specific timelines during the project briefing stage to ensure that your design needs are met promptly.

Can you incorporate our existing brand elements into the design?

+

Absolutely! We understand the importance of maintaining brand consistency. If you have existing brand elements such as logos, colour schemes, or fonts, we will seamlessly incorporate them into the design. Our goal is to create designs that align with your brand identity and reinforce its visual presence.

Do you provide printing services for design projects?

+

While our primary focus is on graphic design, we can assist you with recommendations for reliable printing services. We can guide you in selecting suitable printing options, materials, and finishes that will bring your designs to life. However, please note that printing costs and arrangements are separate from our graphic design services.

Will I have ownership of the design files once the project is completed?

+

Yes, upon project completion and full payment, you will have full ownership of the design files. We will provide you with the necessary file formats and open files so that you can utilize the designs across various platforms and mediums as per your requirements.

What if I have revisions or changes after the design is finalized?

+

We understand that design preferences can evolve, and revisions may be needed. We typically include a certain number of revision rounds in our project proposals to accommodate changes. However, extensive revisions beyond the agreed-upon scope may require additional fees. We strive to maintain open communication throughout the design process to ensure your satisfaction with the final outcome.
